Get ready to pamper your fur baby with the ultimate playtime getaway! Say goodbye to boring indoor days and watch your pup’s tail wag with excitement here at Paws & Play! When the weather is nice, we offer just under half an acre of space for your pup to run, explore, and have a blast. Our yard is securely enclosed with 6-ft fencing, and playtime is always supervised. On indoor days, we have a spacious area filled with plenty of toys for dogs to socialize and have fun. Book now and treat your furry friend to a day full of tail-wagging fun!

Paws & Play is a farmhouse that has been renovated specifically for the dogs. We have 6 different cage-free lounges that we use to separate dogs based on size, energy, and/or temperament.


Our layout allows us to place dogs together who are a good match, ensuring a fun and controlled experience for your pup! Each lounge is complete with couches, dog beds, blankets, chew toys, food, and water, all customized to your dog’s needs. We aim to recreate an in-home experience.
